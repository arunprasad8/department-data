================== ACCESS LEVELS ====================

ADMIN (HOD/Program Coordinator):
- View ALL subjects across ALL classes within the Computer Science department
- Access to all dashboards and reports
- Can modify settings and view analytics
- Full system access

CLASS TEACHER (CT):
- View ALL subjects ONLY for their assigned class
- Cannot access other classes
- Can see all students and their marks in all subjects of their class
- Restricted to class-level access

TEACHER:
- View ONLY their assigned subject
- Cannot access other subjects
- Can see marks only for students in their subject
- Restricted to subject-level access
